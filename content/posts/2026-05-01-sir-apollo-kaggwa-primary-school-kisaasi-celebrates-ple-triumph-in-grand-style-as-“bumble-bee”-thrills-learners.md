Sir Apollo Kaggwa Primary School Kisaasi brought Term One of 2026 to a jubilant and memorable close, staging a vibrant celebration marked by pride, unity, and excitement following an impressive performance in the 2025 Primary Leaving Examinations (PLE). The entire school community, pupils, alumni, parents, staff, and well-wishers, came together to commemorate the milestone in a colorful event that blended achievement with festivity.

![](/img/1001760383.jpg)

The day commenced on an energetic note with a lively aerobics session that saw participants enthusiastically engage in physical activity, setting a spirited tone for the celebrations ahead. This was followed by a grand procession through Kyanja town, led by a vibrant brass band whose rhythmic tunes and coordinated display captured the attention of the surrounding community. The march not only showcased school pride but also symbolized the institution’s continued commitment to holistic development beyond the classroom.

In his address, the Headteacher, Mr. Tweheyo Sam, applauded the collective effort of pupils, teachers, and parents in attaining commendable academic success. He emphasized the school’s dedication to nurturing disciplined, focused, and well-rounded learners, highlighting the PLE results as a testament to consistent hard work and effective guidance. His remarks were followed by a recognition ceremony in which outstanding academic performers, referred to as the “academic giants”, were celebrated and awarded gifts in appreciation of their excellence and determination.

Adding a rich cultural and social flavor to the occasion, a bull roasting session brought members of the school community together in shared celebration, reinforcing bonds and creating a festive atmosphere. Meanwhile, the children were treated to a variety of recreational activities that turned the school grounds into a hub of joy and laughter. From bouncing castles to trampolines, the learners fully immersed themselves in the fun-filled environment.

![](/img/1001760366.jpg)

A standout attraction of the day was the ever-exciting “Bumble Bee,” whose presence sparked immense delight among the children. The lively entertainment feature became a highlight of the celebration, drawing cheers and endless excitement as pupils eagerly participated in the thrilling experience.

![](/img/1001760362.jpg)

![](/img/1001760358.jpg)

As one of the final highlights of the day, alumni joined the school community for a symbolic cake-cutting ceremony, adding a nostalgic and unifying touch to the celebration. This moment not only honored the school’s legacy but also strengthened the bond between past and present learners, inspiring current pupils to uphold the institution’s proud tradition of excellence.

![](/img/1001760377.jpg)

As the curtain fell on the day’s events, it was evident that Sir Apollo Kaggwa Primary School Kisaasi had not only celebrated academic excellence but had also reaffirmed its commitment to creating a balanced and joyful learning environment. The celebration served as both a reward for hard work and a motivation for even greater achievements in the future.
